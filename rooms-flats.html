What is the typical rent for a 1BHK in Jaipur?
Depends on locality and furnishing. Unfurnished 1BHK typically ₹6,000-12,000/month, semi-furnished ₹9,000-15,000/month, fully-furnished ₹12,000-22,000/month. Premium areas cost more.
