Highly variable — depends on guest count, venue type and styling. Budget weddings ₹3-7 lakh, mid-range ₹7-20 lakh, premium ₹20 lakh+ with destination/luxury elements. A detailed quote is provided after a free consultation.
Do you arrange both veg and non-veg catering?
Yes — pure veg, jain, non-veg and live counters all available. Menu is customised per family preferences and guest count.
Can I book only some services (e.g., just decoration or just catering)?
Yes. You can book individual services or the full package — whatever you need. There is no obligation to take a bundle.
How early should I book a Jaipur wedding service?
For peak season (Oct-Feb) we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for premium venues and photographers. Off-season bookings can be confirmed within weeks.
